SUSHIN SHYAM

Sushin Shyam is an Indian music director who works primarily in the Tamil and Malayalam film industries. He is best known for his work on the 2018 film Ethir Neechal. He has also worked on several other Tamil films such as Kadaikutty Singam (2018), Maanagaram (2017), and Chennai 600028 II: Second Innings (2016).
